⚠️ Important caveats for Big Fun Pie
🍕
We need to bring our own food
Big Fun Pie is awaiting their food and health permits. You're welcome to bring your own food, juice boxes, and bottled water to serve in the private party room during the 45-min celebration time. We'll need to plan for this.
🎀
No external decorations allowed
The venue does not allow decorations brought from outside. Party room décor is whatever the venue provides. If a themed look matters, Whirlygig includes "Happy Birthday" decorations and Safari Run includes balloons.
🍽️
Plates & utensils are plastic
They provide plastic plates, forks, tissues, and one-time-use paper tablecloths. Not eco-friendly, but perfectly functional. Lemon Tree is the eco-conscious alternative if that matters.
⏱️
Only 45 min in the party room
The 2-hour party is split between play time and 45 minutes in the private party room for cake, food, and celebration. Plan food setup and cake timing accordingly.
